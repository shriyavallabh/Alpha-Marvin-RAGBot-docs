# Go-to-Market Strategy

**Product:** JurisAgent by Alpha Marvin
**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

## GTM Overview

### Strategy in One Sentence

Land with solo practitioners and small firms through accuracy-focused content marketing, expand into mid-market through referrals and case studies, and capture regulated industries with self-hosted deployment.

### Three Growth Phases

| Phase | Timeline | Focus | Channel | Target |
|-------|----------|-------|---------|--------|
| **1. Pilot & Prove** | Months 1–6 | Product-market fit | Direct outreach, warm network | 10 pilot firms, 50 users |
| **2. Self-Serve Launch** | Months 6–12 | Inbound growth | Content, SEO, community | 500 users, $50K MRR |
| **3. Enterprise & Partners** | Months 12–24 | Revenue scale | Outbound sales, channel partners | 2,000 users, $200K MRR |

## Phase 1: Pilot & Prove (Months 1–6)

### Objective
Validate product-market fit with 10 law firms. Collect case studies and testimonials.

### Actions

**Direct Outreach (Warm Network)**

- Reach out to 50 attorneys through personal and advisor networks
- Offer free 3-month pilot with onboarding support
- Target: 10 firms committed, 50 active users

**Legal Tech Meetups & Events**

- Attend/speak at 3–5 local bar association events
- Demo at LegalTech NYC or similar regional conferences
- Focus on the "AI accuracy crisis" narrative — it's timely and newsworthy

**Pilot Program Structure**

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Duration | 3 months |
| Price | Free (in exchange for feedback and permission to use as case study) |
| Onboarding | 1-hour setup call + document upload assistance |
| Check-ins | Bi-weekly 15-min feedback calls |
| Success metric | Attorney uses JurisAgent for 3+ real matters |
| Exit | Convert to paid tier or graceful offboarding |

### Phase 1 Success Criteria

- [ ] 10 firms actively using the product
- [ ] 3+ written testimonials
- [ ] 2+ case studies with measurable time savings
- [ ] NPS score > 40
- [ ] Identified top 3 feature requests for Phase 3

## Phase 2: Self-Serve Launch (Months 6–12)

### Objective
Launch publicly. Build inbound pipeline through content marketing and SEO. Reach 500 paying users.

### Content Marketing Strategy

**Positioning:** "The legal AI you can actually verify"

**Content Pillars:**

| Pillar | Content Types | SEO Keywords |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **AI Accuracy in Legal** | Blog posts, white papers, case analysis | "legal AI hallucination", "AI accuracy for lawyers", "legal AI benchmark" |
| **Practical Legal AI Guides** | How-to articles, video tutorials | "AI contract review", "legal document analysis AI", "RAG for legal" |
| **Data Sovereignty** | White papers, compliance guides | "self-hosted legal AI", "HIPAA legal AI", "on-premise legal AI" |
| **Customer Stories** | Case studies, testimonials, ROI analyses | "legal AI ROI", "law firm AI tools" |

**Content Calendar (Monthly):**

- 4 blog posts (1/week)
- 1 in-depth white paper or benchmark report
- 2 video tutorials (document upload, Q&A walkthrough)
- 4 LinkedIn posts (founder thought leadership)
- 1 webinar or live demo

### Distribution Channels

| Channel | Strategy | Expected Volume |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| **SEO / Blog** | Long-tail keywords around legal AI accuracy | 2,000–5,000 organic visits/mo by Month 12 |
| **LinkedIn** | Founder thought leadership, customer stories | 500–1,000 followers by Month 12 |
| **Legal Twitter/X** | Legal AI community engagement | Brand awareness |
| **Bar Association Newsletters** | Guest articles, sponsored content | Targeted reach |
| **Product Hunt / Hacker News** | Launch day campaign | 1-day traffic spike, early adopters |
| **Legal Subreddits / Forums** | Authentic participation, not spam | Community trust |

### Conversion Funnel

```
Visitor → Free Trial (14 days, 5 docs) → Solo ($49/mo) → Professional ($99/mo)
                                        ↓
                                   Conversion Rate Targets:
                                   Visit → Trial: 5%
                                   Trial → Paid: 25%

```

### Phase 2 Success Criteria

- [ ] 500 paying users

## Phase 3: Enterprise & Partners (Months 12–24)

### Objective
Scale revenue through outbound sales and channel partnerships. Launch self-hosted licensing.

### Outbound Sales

**First Sales Hire**

- 1 Account Executive with legal tech experience
- Focused on mid-market firms (10–50 attorneys) and in-house teams
- Quota: $500K ARR in Year 1

**Sales Process**

| Stage | Activity | Duration |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Prospecting | LinkedIn outreach, conference leads, bar association lists | Ongoing |
| Discovery | 30-min call to understand document workflows and pain points | Week 1 |
| Demo | Live demo with customer's actual documents (sanitized) | Week 2 |
| Pilot | 2-week free trial with 2–3 team members | Weeks 3–4 |
| Close | Proposal, procurement, onboarding | Weeks 5–8 |

### Channel Partnerships

| Partner Type | Example | Value Proposition |
|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Legal practice management** | Clio, PracticePanther, MyCase | Embed JurisAgent Q&A inside their platform; they get stickier product, we get distribution |
| **Legal tech consultants** | Litera, NetDocuments partners | Recommend JurisAgent for document intelligence; referral fee |
| **Bar associations** | State bar associations | Member benefit / group discount; access to 400K+ attorneys |
| **Law school clinics** | Top 50 law schools | Free tier for students; builds pipeline of future customers |

### Self-Hosted Sales

| Target | Approach |
|--------|---------|
| Government legal departments | Respond to RFPs; emphasize FedRAMP-ready deployment |
| Defense contractors | On-premise requirement is non-negotiable; we're the only option |
| Healthcare organizations | HIPAA compliance through data sovereignty |
| EU-based firms | GDPR data residency through self-hosted deployment |

### Phase 3 Success Criteria

- [ ] 2,000 total users
- [ ] $200K MRR ($2.4M ARR)
- [ ] 5+ enterprise/self-hosted deals
- [ ] 2 active channel partnerships
- [ ] Sales cycle < 60 days for mid-market
